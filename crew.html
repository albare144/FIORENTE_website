The Fiorente Crew

In the interest of privacy of crew and guests, Fiorente crew names and credentials are available with key charter agents upon request. The motor yacht Fiorente hosts an elite crew of six with over a hundred years’ combined experience, speaking more than five different languages, and posessing genuine passion for relaxed luxury service. Crew occupation may vary according to needs of the journey and guests.

Ship Captain

Fiorent’s crew is led by our illustrous captain. His long career in yachting has proven him an expert in what makes a journey unforgettable.

Head Chef

Find our chef ruling his kingdom in the galley. His Michelin star- studded past comes the fore as he prepares your superb daily catch fresh from the ocean.

Chief Stewardess
