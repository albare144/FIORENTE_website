Where la Dolce Vita becomes reality

To some of us, the charm of yachting lies in the calming sound of the waves breaking on the coastline, or the hull of one’s yacht cutting through water. To others, living the good life is all about having the people you love by your side, or the elimination of undesired distractions. In all cases, Fiorente is uniquely designed to provide these essential luxuries. From her 0-speed stabilisers to her luxurious suites, from her lavish entertainment facilities to her star-quality crew, Fiorente has mastered the art of la Dolce Vita.

Good wine, good food, good company: Live the dream

Travellers aboard Fiorente know the importance of delicious, nourishing cuisine. What better example than the culinary masterpieces of Fiorente’s own chef. With an eye for presentation and a talent for innovation even the most exacting guests reconise the world-class quality created on demand aboard Fiorente. And when guests or little ones desire something a little more simple and honest, Fiorente’s chef painstakingly prepares blissful versions of their favourite meals.

Fiorente holds the key to unlocking the Mediterranean dream

Not only is the Mediterranean region known for some of the best food in the world, it is also recognised as one of the most culture-rich and historically significant. Fiorente’s guests are uniquely able to sample this rich palate, avoiding crowds by dipping into historic locations as they please. They are guided where desired by Fiorente’s deeply knowledgeable crew. And during classic events like Cannes Film Festival or the Monaco Grand Prix, Fiorente ensures arrival in style.
