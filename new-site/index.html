I. The Yacht

Art meets history, at sea.

From the Amalfi Coast to the Monaco Grand Prix, from Sardinia to the Cannes Film Festival.

There are few activities as truly carefree and inspiring as exploring the seas aboard a large yacht. Fiorente was designed to afford that elusive mix — quality time with companions and family, while giving time and space to oneself.

A journey aboard Fiorente delights any temperament: from satisfying adrenaline junkies with an abundance of water toys, to nourishing even the most exacting gourmand with chef Krzystof's extraordinary culinary masterpieces.

III. Entertainment & Leisure

Two decks, a private cinema, and the sea.

Multiple seating and dining arrangements across two deck areas make Fiorente the classic choice for al fresco living.

A state-of-the-art entertainment system, anchored by Bang & Olufsen, turns movie night into an occasion. Sonos throughout the vessel means the right tune is always playing at the right time — on the sun deck, in the master, or during an evening with friends at the stern.

And when you're ready to leave the deck: an impressive inventory of adventure gear and water toys is enough to excite even the most jaded adrenaline junkie.

IV. Dining

Good wine. Good food. Good company.

Travellers aboard Fiorente know the importance of delicious, nourishing cuisine.

What better example than the culinary masterpieces of Fiorente's own chef. With an eye for presentation and a talent for innovation, even the most exacting guests recognise the world-class quality created — on demand — aboard Fiorente.

And when guests or little ones desire something a little more simple and honest, Fiorente's chef painstakingly prepares blissful versions of their favourite meals.

La Dolce Vita

Where la dolce vita becomes reality.

To some, the charm of yachting lies in the calming sound of the waves breaking against the hull — the sea cutting through itself.

To others, the good life is the people you love by your side, and the quiet removal of distractions.

Fiorente is designed for both.
